Why Choose Us for Sub-Zero Repair?

Sub-Zero refrigerators are built to last 20+ years, and many units we service are still running strong after decades. These aren't disposable appliances—they're investments worth maintaining properly. When your Sub-Zero needs service, you need technicians who specialize in these premium units.

Our technicians understand Sub-Zero's unique dual refrigeration system that uses separate compressors and evaporators for the refrigerator and freezer sections. We know how their air purification system works, how to diagnose sealed system problems, and how to service units from the newest models back to legacy units from the 1990s and earlier.

We serve all of Westchester County including Scarsdale, Bronxville, Rye, Purchase, Harrison, Bedford, Chappaqua, Pound Ridge, Armonk, White Plains, New Rochelle, Larchmont, Mamaroneck, Tarrytown, and surrounding communities. Sub-Zero refrigerators are common in the high-end homes throughout the county, and we're the local experts homeowners trust.

The most common Sub-Zero service need is condenser cleaning—something that should happen every 6-12 months but often gets neglected. A dirty condenser makes the compressor work harder and can lead to premature failure. We clean condensers, replace worn gaskets, repair ice makers, diagnose temperature problems, and handle everything from simple maintenance to major sealed system repairs.
